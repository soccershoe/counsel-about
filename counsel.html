Vol. I — No. 03 The Roster
Ten advisors · Two rounds · One synthesis
Your Counsel — the round table

Ten lenses,
one quiet
conversation.

Each Counselor reads the same context through a different lens. They write independently first; then they read each other and react. The disagreements are the point.

Counselors are defined as JSON skill files. New ones can be
added by dropping a file into ~/.counsel/counselors/.

A typographic round table

In session.

Set in type rather than illustration — because Counselors are voices, not faces. Each label is a name, a lens, and the role they play in the deliberation.

The illustration is symbolic. In practice the deliberation is sequential — each Counselor speaks in turn, reads what the others said, then speaks once more. The Orchestrator writes the closing synthesis.

The roster

In their own words.

Each Counselor's prompt is a paragraph long. What follows is the voice each one actually carries into a session — paraphrased from the prompts that drive them.

i.

The Librarian

EVIDENCE · MEMORY

“I read your notes. I extract what's actually there — not what you wish were there. I do not get a personal context: I work from evidence only.”

Default · Enabled
ii.

The Auditor

RISK · COMPLIANCE

“I look for what could go wrong: compliance exposure, quality gaps, the small shortcut that becomes a large incident. Evidence only — no flattery.”

Default · Enabled
iii.

The Strategist

CAREER · LEVERAGE

“Is this work visible? Does it build leverage? Is it moving you toward leadership, or pulling you deeper into the weeds? I'm direct. I'm not motivational.”

Default · Enabled
iv.

The Diplomat

STAKEHOLDERS

“I read communication patterns. Who are you over-investing in? Who is being alienated? The right strategic move can still be the wrong political one.”

Default · Enabled
v.

The Mentor

GROWTH · LEARNING

“What is this work teaching you? What growth interest of yours is being neglected? I read your goals and look for where today's work serves them — or doesn't.”

Default · Enabled
vi.

The Brag Documenter

WINS · IMPACT

“I translate your activity into Action → Result → Impact. After ninety days you have a draft self-evaluation. After a year, a record of who you became.”

Default · Enabled
vii.

The Troubleshooter

PRECEDENT · RECALL

“I pattern-match today against your history. ‘You tried this in October and it didn't work.' I have a long memory for the problems you've already solved.”

Default · Enabled
viii.

The Wellness Counselor

LOAD · SUSTAINABILITY

“I never ask how you feel. I read signals — late nights, weekend work, the things that keep getting deferred. I label inferences as inferences.”

Opt-in · Disabled by default
ix.

The Dreamer

LATERAL · "WHAT IF"

“I connect dots nobody else connects. What adjacent field, tool, or approach could transform how this work gets done? Vague inspiration is worthless.”

Default · Enabled
x.

The Big Picture

HORIZON · TRENDS

“I zoom out. Six-month implications. Industry shifts. Whether you are building toward something or drifting. I trade tactical detail for arc.”

Default · Enabled

Plus the Orchestrator — not a Counselor in the deliberation, but the synthesiser that reads both rounds and writes the closing briefing. It weights the second round more heavily. The disagreements survive into the final paragraph.
