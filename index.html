Ten quiet advisors,
watching only your work,

Counsel is a fully local, privacy-first system that watches the context of your work and convenes a panel of AI advisors to deliberate on it — every day, every week, every quarter. Their findings go to one person: you.

I.

The surveillance model of workplace AI treats employees as resources to be measured and optimised from above. Counsel rejects that premise entirely.

You already have the intelligence — a decade of pattern-matching, hard-won judgment, institutional memory. None of it compounds. Every day you start over, every review you scramble. Counsel is a system that watches patiently, remembers everything, and gives the picture back to you.

What you get out of it

Self-knowledge that
survives the week.

Counsel does not optimise you. It does not score you. It returns to you the picture of your own work that you've never had time to assemble.

i.

Clarity at the end of the day.

A daily briefing reads like a thoughtful colleague who watched your day and summarises it back. What happened. What was deferred again. What the shape of tomorrow ought to be. No metrics. No nudges.

ii.

A pre-written self-review.

The Brag Documenter quietly translates day-to-day work into Action → Result → Impact. After a quarter, you have a draft self-evaluation. After a year, a record of who you became.

iii.

An honest career read.

The Strategist tracks whether you are doing senior-level work or sliding into maintenance toil. The Diplomat reads your communication patterns. They disagree with each other, often, in your favour.

iv.

Patterns you cannot see.

Weekly, monthly, quarterly, yearly. Each cadence reads the previous summaries, not the raw notes — so it sees arcs, not incidents. The thing you've been deferring for three weeks is now the headline.

v.

A record only you read.

Every briefing lives on your machine. SQLite. Markdown. Yours to export, delete, archive. There is no cloud account. There is no team dashboard looking down. There is no leaderboard.

vi.

Compounding context.

Each Counselor remembers what they said last time, and what their colleagues said. After thirty days you stop getting briefings and start getting an evolving conversation about your career.
